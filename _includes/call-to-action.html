We're proud of our Allotment!

Our community allotment is a shared area where individuals or groups have space to grow their own fruits, vegetables, herbs, and flowers. As well as helping people to connect with each other and with the natural world. We aim to run projects and educational programs, such as teaching children about where their food comes from and the importance of sustainable agriculture. Our plot holders are made up of all ages and backgrounds, whether you are an experienced gardener or just starting out, this really is a community allotment.
